Perfect Potato Soup

How to Make The Best Potato Soup

Potato Soup loaded with chunks of tender, hearty potatoes and made with a rich and creamy soup base. This soup is a cook's favorite and for good reason – it’s easy to make and absolutely delicious! Top it with cheddar and bacon and no one will be able to resist.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Water
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Prep potatoes by scrubbing your potatoes clean, peeling, and then cutting them into about 2-inch chunks. Place the potatoes into a large soup pot (I use my Dutch oven) and cover with clean water and stir.
  2. Cook over medium heat until the potatoes are fork tender, about 20 minutes. Remove from the heat and drain away the water from the potatoes.
  3. Add your whole milk and butter to the potatoes and place back on medium heat. Allow the milk to bubble slightly around the edges of the soup pot, about 5 to 10 minutes.
  4. Remove from the heat and season with salt and pepper to taste.
